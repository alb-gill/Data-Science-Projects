# Data-Science-Projects
•	Detected operational risks in ship engine data using anomaly detection techniques, including IQR, One-Class SVM, and Isolation Forest, to identify abnormal behaviour and support predictive maintenance

•	Developed customer segmentation with clustering using K-Means Clustering, Hierarchical Clustering and t-SNE to identify high-value customer groups and improving marketing targeting strategies

•	Developed supervised learning models to predict student dropout risk, enabling early identification of at-risk students and targeted retention interventions

•	Built Time-series forecasting models for sales and demand prediction, supporting inventory optimi-sation and strategic business planning

•	Applied Natural Language Processing (NLP) for topic modelling techniques to real-world text da-tasets, uncovering key themes and actionable insights from unstructured data

•	Developed market forecasting models for car manufacturing and distribution across South American countries using SARIMAX, LSTM, XGBoost and TimeGPT to support strategic decision-making and market planning
